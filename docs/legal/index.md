# Legal and compliance

VeriDoc is committed to transparency in how we handle your data and operate
our service. This section contains all legal documents governing the use of
the VeriDoc automated documentation platform.

## Legal documents

| Document | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| [Terms of service](terms-of-service.md) | Subscription terms, usage limits, acceptable use, and liability |
| [Privacy policy](privacy-policy.md) | Data collection, processing, storage, retention, and your GDPR rights |
| [Data processing agreement](data-processing-agreement.md) | GDPR-compliant DPA with sub-processor list and breach notification procedures |
| [Security policy](security-policy.md) | Infrastructure security, encryption, authentication, and incident response |

## Quick reference

**Data location:** Hetzner Cloud, Germany (EU)

**Encryption:** TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest

**Password security:** PBKDF2-SHA256, 600,000 iterations

**Data retention:** Account data kept 30 days after closure, then permanently deleted

**LLM processing:** Opt-in only. Disable AI features to keep all content on VeriDoc infrastructure.

**GDPR rights:** Access, correct, delete, export, restrict, object. Email <privacy@veri-doc.app>.
