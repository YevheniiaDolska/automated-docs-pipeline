# VeriOps one-pager

## Problem

Engineering teams ship fast. Documentation drifts, support load increases, and AI assistants answer from inconsistent sources.

## What VeriOps does

VeriOps installs and operationalizes an automated documentation pipeline inside client constraints (cloud, hybrid, or strict-local/air-gapped).

You get:

1. Automated docs generation and updates from real engineering inputs.
1. API-first multi-protocol flow (REST, GraphQL, gRPC, AsyncAPI, WebSocket).
1. Quality gates and recurring operational reports.
1. Optional RAG layer on top of prepared, validated knowledge.

## Why this is different

1. Quality is hardened before indexing (not only retrieval tuning).
1. Contradictions and stale content are detected before RAG corruption.
1. Critical conflicts can be excluded from retrieval index automatically.
1. Runtime guardrails reduce low-confidence hallucinations.
