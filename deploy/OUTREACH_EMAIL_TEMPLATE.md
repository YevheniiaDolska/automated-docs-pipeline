# Outreach email template

## Subject options

1. `{Company}: quick docs risk snapshot + 20-minute walkthrough`
1. `Found a high-impact docs gap in {Company} public docs`
1. `{Company} docs ops audit: evidence attached`

## Email body

Hi {FirstName},

I ran a targeted documentation audit for {Company} and found a few concrete risk points in the current public docs flow.

Top finding:

- {one specific issue with evidence}

Business impact estimate:

- {one quantified number from scorecard}

I attached:

1. Executive PDF audit
1. HTML scorecard with traceable metrics

If useful, I can walk your team through this in 20 minutes and give a staged remediation plan (quick wins first, then deeper automation).

Book a slot:

- {Calendly URL}

Best,
{YourName}

## Rules

1. Keep email under 180 words.
1. Mention one finding, not ten.
1. Always include a clear CTA with one calendar link.
1. Do not promise custom implementation in first email.
